New Features In ElasticBox

Docker as a Service

If you’re using Docker or curious about it, we have good news for you! You can now use Docker in ElasticBox as a service for another means to deliver applications and services to your cloud resources. With this integration, we’re making it easier than ever for you to add Docker to your DevOps toolkit.

If you use AWS, we’ve just made it even easier for you to use their great features such as IAM and ARN in ElasticBox. Now you can use ARN to connect your AWS account to ElasticBox and deploy applications with even greater administrative controls.
